Popular locations in India
From swanky upscale restaurants to the cosiest hidden gems serving the most incredible food, Zomato covers it all. Explore menus, and millions of restaurant photos and reviews from users just like you, to find your next great meal.
Bengaluru, also known as the Silicon Valley of India, is a melting pot of cultures. The city's food scene reflects this diversity. From traditional South Indian fare to global cuisines, Bengaluru has it all. Don't miss out on iconic restaurants like Mavalli Tiffin Room (MTR) for mouthwatering dosas or Windmills Craftworks for a fine dining experience amidst nature.

Agra, famous for the enchanting Taj Mahal, is also a treat for food enthusiasts. hile visiting this historical city, savor Mughlai delights at Pinch of Spice or indulge in Agra's renowned petha (a sweet delicacy) at Panchhi Petha



Chennai's cuisine is a celebration of flavors, with a focus on seafood and South Indian delicacies. Explore Saravana Bhavan for delectable vegetarian dishes or try the famous Chettinad cuisine at Anjappar. For a taste of Chennai's unique filter coffee, head to Sangeeth

.


Known for its sweet roshogollas and iconic street food, Kolkata's culinary landscape is a gastronomic adventure. Experience the grandeur of traditional Bengali dishes at 6 Ballygunge Place or enjoy the puchka (pani puri) at Vivekananda Park.


Mumbai, the city that never sleeps, offers a wide variety of dining options. From vada pav at Ashok Vadapav to the fine dining experience at The Table, Mumbai caters to every taste. Don't forget to try the seafood at Trishna for an authentic coastal experience. .


Pune is not just an educational hub but also a hub for delicious food. Visit Vaishali for a South Indian breakfast or enjoy an exquisite North Indian meal at Kayani Bakery. The city's vibrant street food scene is a must-try, with stalls serving misal pav and bhel puri. .


Vellore, a city in Tamil Nadu, offers a unique blend of Tamil and Andhra cuisines. Try the fiery Andhra biryani at Nagarjuna or explore the local flavors at Arya Bhavan. The city's food scene may be less known, but it's worth exploring. .


Salem, nestled in the heart of Tamil Nadu, is known for its simple yet delicious cuisine. Visit Shree Anandha Bhavan for authentic South Indian meals or try the local street food like kuzhi paniyaram.
